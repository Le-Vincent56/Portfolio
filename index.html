ABOUT ME

My name is Vincent Le and I am currently a third-year Game Design & Development (GDD) student at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). My interests for game design include Graphics and Engine Programming, Game Audio Programming, Sound Design and Soundtrack Composition, and Narrative Design. My multitude of interests within game design stems from one core belief: Games should not just be fun, they should be worthwhile. As a long-time musician and storyteller, I have found that when these two art forms are used well within a game, then the overall experience is much more enjoyable to the player. That is my goal: to make a worthwhile, fun, and intuitive experience for players that will hopefully change their perspective on certain themes, cultures, or experiences after playtime.

NOTE: The HTML, CSS, and JavaScript of this website was all designed and created by me!

TRIPARTITE

GENRE:

Text-based, Interactive Story

Tripartite is an interactive fiction work that revolves around Dane, an amnesiac trapped in a blind world, and three voices, Egor, Ida, and Summer. As an attempt to understand context-aware dialogue systems, Tripartite follows a branching narrative as the user makes choices that influence the story and the dialogue that is said during it.

ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Created a C# (Unity)-based interactive fiction story as a solo dev
  • Implemented a Fuzzy Pattern Matching, Event-Based dialogue system
  • Wrote and designed narrative characters and structure
  • Coded many UI-based scripts to help with UI/UX design

PIXIJSNAKE

PIXIJSnake is a recreation of the classic Snake arcade game using JavaScript and the PIXIJS Graphics Framework. The player can move the Snake through it's head, and the body will follow. As the player collects food, their score will increase, but the snake will also get longer. One loses once the Snake collides with itself or the game window's boundaries.

ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Used JavaScript to interact and functionally use the PIXIJS Graphics Framework to display a game
  • Recreated the classic Snake arcade game using JavaScript
  • Used HTML5 and CSS to create a functional structure of a website and a professional design.

A LION'S DANCE

SEPTEMBER 2023 - PRESENT

GENRE:

Military Fantasy/Historical Fiction

Vinh's ordinary life in South Quhoung is lifted out of his hands and into the gauntlets of war. Struggling with the cruelty of war, the weight of grief, and the burden of revenge, Vinh attempts to collect the remnants of his life and piece it back together by force, or by victory. Inspired by the events of the Vietnam War, A Lion's Dance imitates the Vietnamese experience within South Vietnam through the lens of fantasy. In addition to spreading knowledge of the Vietnamese experience and culture, A Lion's Dance attempts to unravel the themes of the innate villainy of war and its escalating and lasting consequences.
